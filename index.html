ABOUT NCGIET-National Conference

on Global Innovations in Engineering & Technology

      The conference will be held at GIET-(AUTONOMOUS) Rajahmundry, East Godavari District, Andhra Pradesh, India on March 20th & 21th 2020. The aim objective of NCGIET is to provide a world class platform to present and discuss all the latest research and results of researchers related Big Data, IoT, Cyber Security,Information Technology and many more.This conference provides opportunities for the different areas delegates to exchange new ideas and application experiences face to face, to establish business or research relations and to find global partners for future collaboration.
We hope that the conference results constituted significant contribution to the knowledge in these up to date scientific field.The organizing committee of conference is pleased to invite prospective authors to submit their original manuscripts to NCGIET'2020.

      This prestigious event is organized with a motivation to provide an excellent national platform for the students,academicians, researchers, engineers & industrial participants around the world to SHARE their research findings with the global experts. The conference will be held every year to make it an ideal platform for people to share views and experiences in Computer Science and Information Technology related areas.

RULES AND REGULATIONS (Read Carefully)

For paper Submission and Publication in NCGIET National Conference:
      01 - All Paper should be the original one and it should not be published in any manner before and after the conference.
      02 - All Authors and Co-authors must inform their respective Dept. Head/Principal/Guide about the paper submission of papers in to NCGIET National Conference.
      03 - All Authors and Co-authors should write the correct affiliation inside the paper.
      04 - The Organizing Committee may not accept the papers submitted after the last date of registration.
      05 - NCGIET accepts each paper for conference after doing the review by three NCGIET Reviewers.
      06 - NCGIET never publishes the author's or co-author's emails online.
      07 - NCGIET cannot add, modify or delete any authors or co-authors name inside the paper after the registration of the paper without the permission of all authors and co-authors present inside the paper.
      08 - NCGIET never publishes any unregistered paper.
      09 - NCGIET is not responsible for identifying the original author or co-author of the paper. The person who submits the paper will be taken as the original author of the paper.
      10 - In any case if NCGIET receives the complaint about the originality of the author and it found to be true the paper will be suspended from the publication immediately.
      11 - NCGIET cannot republish or distribute any suspended paper from the date of suspension.

AUTHOR GUIDELINES

    The paper must be as per the IEEE Format only.
    All submitted articles should report original, previously unpublished research results, experimental or theoretical. Articles submitted to the Conference should meet these criteria and must not be under consideration for publication elsewhere. Manuscripts should follow the style of the Conference and are subject to both review and editing.

SUBMISSION METHOD

For paper Submission and Publication in NCGIET National Conference:
      01 - First register yourself by pressing the register button which redirects you to a Google form.

      02 - You will then recieve a mail with your Unique Registration Number from our maintainence team.
      03 - Then, click on paper submission tab below the registration tab which redirects you to another Google Form.
      04 - Fill the form and upload your paper in .doc or .pdf format along with Plagiarism check percentage and document of plagiarism.
      05 - Then our maintainence team will undergo the scrutiny procedure and give you the status through your mails.
